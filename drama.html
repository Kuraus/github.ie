Release Date: Released

Synopsis: Adam begins to take an interest in his neighbor, the enigmatic Harry. As their relationship deepens, Adam returns to the house he grew up in, where his parents, who died 30 years ago, seem to live as if nothing has changed.

Bob Marley: One Love

Director: Reinaldo Marcus Green

Starring: Kingsley Ben-Adir

Release Date: Released

Synopsis: The story of how reggae icon Bob Marley overcame adversity and the journey behind his revolutionary music.

Wicked Little Letters

Director: Thea Sharrock

Starring: Olivia Colman

Release Date: Released

Synopsis: 1920s. In the coastal town of Littlehampton, the devout Edith Swan (Olivia Colman) and other local residents begin to receive indiscreet letters written in language as eccentric as it is obscene. Suspicion quickly falls on Rose Gooding (Jessie Buckley), an impetuous neighbor who sees her freedom and the custody of her daughter in jeopardy due to these accusations. While the letters continue to plague the population, police officer Gladys Moss (Anjana Vasan) will put her wits to work to solve the mystery and catch the real culprit of this whole mess.

Cabrini

Director: Rod Barr, Alejandro Monteverde

Starring: Cristiana Dell'Anna

Release Date: Released

Synopsis: After an Italian nun moves to New York City, Francesca Cabrini fights the Archbishop, the mayor, and obvious xenophobia to build an orphanage for impoverished Italian-Americans. With entrepreneurial spirit, determination, and courage, Cabrini creates a home filled of compassion and a legacy that will last forever.

Seize Them

Director: Curtis Vowell

Starring: Nicola Coughlan

Release Date: Released

Synopsis: Britain of the Middle Ages, Queen Dagan is overthrown by a revolution led by the humble Joan. The queen becomes a fugitive in her own land and must face hardships and dangers to regain her throne.

Chalengers

Director: Luca Gaudagnino

Starring: Mike Faist

Release Date: 26/04

Synopsis: From visionary filmmaker Luca Guadagnino, Challengers stars Zendaya as Tashi Duncan, a former tennis prodigy turned coach and a force of nature who makes no apologies for her game on and off the court. Married to a champion on a losing streak (Mike Faist - West Side Story), Tashi's strategy for her husband's redemption takes a surprising turn when he must face off against the washed-up Patrick (Josh O'Connor - The Crown) - his former best friend and Tashi's former boyfriend. As their pasts and presents collide, and tensions run high, Tashi must ask herself, what will it cost to win.

Wicked

Director: Jon M. Chu

Starring: Cynthia Erivo

Release Date: 28/11

Synopsis: Wicked tells the story of Elphaba, the future Wicked Witch of the West and her relationship with Glinda, the Good Witch of the North. Their friendship struggles through their opposing personalities and viewpoints, rivalry over the same love-interest, their reactions to the Wizard's corrupt government, and, ultimately, Elphaba's public fall from grace. The plot is set mostly before Dorothy's arrival from Kansas, and includes several references to well-known scenes and dialogue in the 1939 film The Wizard of Oz as a backstory.
