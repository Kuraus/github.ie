Argyle

Director: Rose Glass

Starring: Anna Baryshnikov

Release Date: 03/05

Synopsis: It is a co-production between the United Kingdom and the United States. The film stars Kristen Stewart, Katy O'Brian, Jena Malone, Anna Baryshnikov, Dave Franco, and Ed Harris. Kingdom of The Planet of The Apes

Director: Wes Ball

Starring: Freya Allan

Release Date: 10/05

Synopsis: Set 300 years after Caesar's death, the film follows Noa, a young ape discovering Caesar's legacy. The main antagonist is Proximus Caesar, a tyrannical ape leader. The story explores themes of legacy, leadership, and the future of both species.

Furiosa

Director: George Miller

Starring: Anya Taylor-Joy

Release Date: 24/05

Synopsis: As the world falls, young Furiosa is snatched from the Green Place of Many Mothers into the hands of a great biker horde led by the warlord Dementus. Sweeping through the wasteland, they encounter the citadel presided over by Immortan Joe. The two tyrants wage war for dominance, and Furiosa must survive many trials as she puts together the means to find her way home.
