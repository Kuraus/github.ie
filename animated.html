Migration

Director: Benjamin Renner, Guylo Homsy

Starring: Kumail Nanjiani

Release Date: Released

Synopsis: From the creators of Despicable Me comes an adventure-filled new comedy about overcoming your fears and opening yourself up to the world and its opportunities, filled with Illumination's signature subversive humor, authentic heart and unforgettable characters. The Mallard family embarks on a journey south for the winter to Jamaica via New York City, only for their well-laid plans to go awry, leading to new friends and unknown horizons.

Butterfly Tale

Director: Sophie Roy

Starring: Tatiana Maslany

Release Date: Released

Synopsis: A brave and adorable one-winged butterfly named Patrick hides in a trailer to take the trip of a lifetime. With his best friend, a caterpillar named Marty, and Jennifer, a butterfly who fears heights, Patrick will become an unexpected hero. But to do so he must face his greatest fear: accept his uniqueness to triumph over adversity while fighting against changes in weather patterns, humans and three evil birds bent on seeking revenge.

Kung Fu Panda 4

Director: Mike Mitchel, Stephanie Stine

Starring: Jack Black

Release Date: Released

Synopsis: Po is training a new warrior to take his place so that he can become the spiritual leader of the Valley of Peace. However, the appearance of an evil sorceress who can change her appearance disrupts their plans.

The Garfield Movie

Director: Mark Dindal

Starring: Samuel L. Jackson

Release Date: 24/05

Synopsis: After an unexpected reunion with Garfield's long-lost father - the cat Vic - he and Odie are forced to abandon their pampered life to join Vic in a high-stakes heist.

Inside Out 2

Director: Kelsey Mann

Starring: Amy Poehler

Release Date: 14/06

Synopsis: Teenager Riley's mind headquarters is undergoing a sudden demolition to make room for something entirely unexpected: new Emotions. Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear and Disgust, who've long been running a successful operation by all accounts, aren't sure how to feel when Anxiety shows up. And it looks like she's not alone.

Despicable Me 4

Director: Chris Renauld

Starring: Steve Carrel

Release Date: 05/07

Synopsis: Gru, Lucy, Margo, Edith, and Agnes welcome a new member to the family, Gru Jr., who is intent on tormenting his dad. Gru faces a new nemesis in Maxime Le Mal and his girlfriend Valentina, and the family is forced to go on the run. This daddy's a baddie.

Transformers One

Director: Josh Cooley

Starring: Scarlett Johansson

Release Date: 11/10

Synopsis: Transformers One (2024) will be the first theatrical animated movie in 38 years since the original movie: The Transformers: The Movie (1986). This movie will take place on the planet Cybertron, and will center on the relationship between Optimus Prime and Megatron going from brothers-in-arms to enemies.

The Wild Robot

Director: Chris Sanders

Starring: Lupita Nyong'o

Release Date: 18/10

Synopsis: A shuttle mysteriously washes upon an island covered with animals. A robot named Roz appears out of it and discovers she needs to adapt to survive. She not only gets adjusted to the environment quickly, but makes friendships with the wildlife on the island and adopts an orphaned gosling.

Moana 2

Director: David G. Derrick Jr.

Starring: Auli'i Cravalho

Release Date: 27/11

Synopsis: This takes audiences on an expansive new voyage with Moana, Maui and a brand-new crew of unlikely seafarers. After receiving an unexpected call from her wayfinding ancestors, Moana must journey to the far seas of Oceania and into dangerous, long-lost waters for an adventure unlike anything she's ever faced.

The Lord of the Rings: The War of The Rohirrim

Director: Kenji Kamiyama

Starring: Brian Cox

Release Date: 13/12
