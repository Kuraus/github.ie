Lisa Frankenstein

Director: Zelda Williams

Starring: Kathryn Newton

Release Date: Released

Synopsis: After suffering an unspeakable tragedy, Lisa finds herself at a new school her senior year in 1989, struggling to fit in, despite her "sister" Taffy trying to get her to conform to her more typical cheerleader vibe. When a freak accident reanimates a corpse from the abandoned cemetery where she was spending time, she must keep his arrival a secret from her family and classmates, all while deciding how much she wants to help him, and at what cost.

Ghostbusters: Frozen Empire

Director: Gil Kenan

Starring: McKenna Grace

Release Date:Released

Synopsis: The Spengler family returns to the famous New York City firehouse with the original Ghostbusters. When an ancient artifact unleashes an evil force, ghostbusters new and old must band together to protect the world.

IF

Director: John Krasinski

Starring: Ryan Reynolds

Release Date:17/05

Synopsis: From writer and director John Krasinski, IF is about a girl who discovers that she can see everyone's imaginary friends — and what she does with that superpower — as she embarks on a magical adventure to reconnect forgotten IFs with their kids.

Deadpool 3

Director: Shawn Levy

Starring: Ryan Reynolds

Release Date: 26/07

Synopsis: Wolverine is recovering from his injuries when he crosses paths with the loudmouth, Deadpool, who has traveled back in time to heal him in the hopes of becoming friends and forming a team to take down a common enemy.

BeetleJuice 2

Director: Tim Burton

Starring: Jenna Ortega

Release Date: 06/09

Synopsis: After an unexpected family tragedy, three generations of the Deetz family return home to Winter River. Still haunted by Beetlejuice, Lydia's life is turned upside down when her rebellious teenage daughter, Astrid, discovers the mysterious model of the town in the attic and the portal to the Afterlife is accidentally opened. With trouble brewing in both realms, it's only a matter of time until someone says Beetlejuice's name three times and the mischievous demon returns to unleash his very own brand of mayhem.

Paddington in Peru

Director: Dougal Wilson

Starring: Olivia Colman

Release Date: 08/11

Synopsis: Paddington returns to Peru to visit his beloved Aunt Lucy, who now resides at the Home for Retired Bears. With the Brown Family in tow, a thrilling adventure ensues when a mystery plunges them into an unexpected journey through the Amazon rainforest and up to the mountain peaks of Peru.
